The Major Arcana

The Major Arcana consists of 22 cards and represents the trump cards of the tarot deck. Numbered from 0 to 21 starting with The Fool and ends with The World.

The Minor Arcana

The Minor Arcana consists of 56 cards and represents the suits of Wands, Cups, Swords and Pentacles. Each suit have cards 1 to 10 and court cards of the Page, Knight, Queen and King.
